MATLAB Numerical Analysis Algorithm Design

Designed a numerical simulation to model multiple-beam interference in dielectric slabs. Implemented an iterative algorithm to sum infinite geometric series, analyzing convergence rates to predict high-finesse Fabry-Pérot resonance peaks.

Airport Flow Management Simulation

MATLAB Linear Programming Data Analysis

A quantitative simulation of Air Traffic Flow Management (ATFM) strategies at Adolfo Suárez Madrid-Barajas Airport (MAD). Developed custom GDP and GHP algorithms using linear integer programming to optimize flight slot allocation under reduced capacity constraints. The study compares operational costs against delay metrics and proposes an intermodal rail substitution model to reduce CO₂ emissions.

Airspace Sectorization Optimization

MATLAB Genetic Algorithms Pareto Optimization

An optimization study of the Barcelona Flight Information Region (FIR) using Genetic Algorithms (GA) to balance Air Traffic Control workload. Implemented a multi-objective function to minimize conflict points and sector crossings, generating Pareto Fronts to identify the optimal trade-off solutions. The study analyzed 6 complexity parameters to determine the ideal sector configuration.
